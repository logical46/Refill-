The Problem

Most restaurants lose 40–60% of customers quietly — and never know why.

A customer visits your restaurant, enjoys the meal, and leaves satisfied. Then life gets busy. They don't come back next week, or the week after. No complaint was made. No bad review was left. They simply moved on — because nobody followed up.

This is the silent revenue leak that kills repeat business. And your staff are too busy running service to track and re-engage customers manually.

📉
Lost revenue, not lost taste Inactive customers didn't stop liking your food. They stopped being reminded to come back.
⏱️
Staff have zero bandwidth Your team is focused on cooking, serving, and managing daily operations. Customer retention becomes nobody's job.
🔕
Silence is not loyalty A customer who visited 3 months ago is not a loyal customer. They are a cold lead — and cold leads go to competitors.
💸
Acquiring new customers costs 5x more Retaining an existing customer is far cheaper than finding a new one. Yet most restaurants put all energy into acquisition.
The Solution

Refill is your restaurant's automated retention engine.
