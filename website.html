How It Works

Four steps.
Fully automatic.

01
📋
Customer Signs Up
Customers fill a branded form when they visit — name, phone, food preferences, birthday. Takes 30 seconds.
02
📍
Every Visit Tracked
Staff logs each visit in seconds using a simple check-in page. The system updates the customer's last visit date automatically.
03
🧠
System Detects Cold Customers
Every morning Refill scans your customer list. Anyone who hasn't visited in 30 days gets flagged automatically.
04
💬
WhatsApp Sent Automatically
A personalised WhatsApp message is sent to each cold customer — using their name and their favourite food. No manual work needed.
