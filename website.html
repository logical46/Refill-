How It Works

Four steps.
Fully automatic.

Customer Signs Up
Customers fill a branded digital form on their first visit — name, WhatsApp number, food preferences, birthday. Takes just 30 seconds.
02

Every Visit Tracked
Staff logs each customer visit using a simple check-in page. The last visit date updates automatically in the system.
03

Every morning Refill scans your entire customer list. Anyone who hasn't visited in 30 days is automatically flagged as cold.
